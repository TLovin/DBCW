Information System - MovieLens dataset

About

Are you a marketing professional in the movie industry?

If so, then this information system is made for you! It uses the MovieLens dataset containing information about movies, ratings and tags of movies provided by viewers, etc. The purpose of this system is to help you analyse how viewers have responded to movies, to help you understand the market for movies you are planning and to help you understand the different kinds of viewers of movies and their varying preferences.
